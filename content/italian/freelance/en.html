Freelance Carreer

Beginning

In 2018 I took up programming again, adapting to the leap that technology has made during the four years I taught. After a period of research, I decided to dedicate myself to php. I then learned how to use WordPress and Laravel and have created my own CMS in core php, a project I had in mind from the beginning.

First Jobs

In 2019 I started to get serious about it. By this time I had gotten the hang of it and was looking for my first clients. I also started making some side-projects to improve my skills and present my work. During this period I made the website of the sports association Io mi move, of the rendering studio "Studio MAC", of the record label Brigante Records (first work in vanilla Javascript) and other small realities.

A small step forward

In mid-2019 I got in touch with Fei Studio and the Federcasa Association, with whom I collaborated to create their online presence, creating their websites and social profiles, and creating the website of the newspaper "The Landlord."

Bikeitalia.it

At the end of 2019, I made a real breakthrough. I got in touch with Bikenomist, the company that runs Bikeitalia.it and who entrusted me with the task of creating the new theme for their website. After creating the new site and e-commerce, I continued the collaboration by developing the new e-learning platform. I continue to collaborate with them.

Podcast

A passion for web development led me to the creation of the podcast Code and Design where I recount my adventures in the world of programming. I really like the gentle and intimate format of the podcast; it has slowly become my corner of reflections and often the cradle of ideas.

Education

I have started to dedicate section of this website to some Guides on web development, where I would like to share my knowledge. I have learned a lot thanks to open source communities around the world and would like to give something back in my own small way.

Mountains

Ho sempre amato la montagna, sotto tutti i punti di vista. Da sempre penso che sia un luogo magico e meraviglioso, in cui fare sport ma anche da vivere, un luogo in cui immergersi completamente. Dal 2020 ho sviluppato, gestisco e contribuisco al progetto AttrezzaturaTrekking.it, un portale di consigli sul trekking ma non solo, anche di promozione della vita in montagna. Grazie a questo progetto posso unire passione e lavoro.
